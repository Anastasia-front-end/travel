Most popular places

MarrakeshMorocco

Marrakech is the №1 tourist destination according to Travelers' Choice 2015. It is a magical place with many markets, gardens, palaces and mosques. While you get to know the narrow courtyards and wander the streets of the historic part of the city, the day will pass unnoticed. Find peace in the serene gardens of Majorelle or enjoy the beauty of the city's ancient mosques.

Siem ReapCambodia

As the morning light floods the verdant temples and ruins of Angkor Wat, an ordinary dawn over the city of Siem Reap seems like an unforgettable sight. This temple complex is rightfully considered one of the largest in the world. Together with the "great capital", the city of Angkor Thom, built in the 12th century, it is the main reason for visiting the city of Siem Reap.

IstanbulTurkey

Istanbul, which took third place this year, is where Europe and Asia meet: magnificent ancient architecture coexists with modern restaurants and the city's nightlife. Mosques, bazaars and Turkish baths of Istanbul will not let you get bored. The first thing to visit is the Sultan-Ahmed-Kami Mosque (Blue Mosque), which can be seen from different parts of the city.
